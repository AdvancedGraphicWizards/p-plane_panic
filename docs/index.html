About

Team up and fly a plane together by balancing your phones in this co-op game.

See how far you can fly without running out of fuel or catching on fire!

How to Play

Join a Team

Gather your friends and form a team to start your adventure.

Balance Your Phones

Work together by balancing your phones to control the plane.

Avoid Obstacles

Navigate through the skies while avoiding obstacles and hazards.

Reach New Heights

See how far you can fly without running out of fuel or catching fire!
